Understood. Based on your requirement, this will be a **multi-account email CRM/dashboard** instead of Excel upload.

### Final system architecture

#### Authentication

* **1 Admin account**
* **5–6 sender accounts** (USA, UK, India, etc.)
* Role-based login

Example:

* Admin
* USA Team
* UK Team
* India Team
* UAE Team
* Australia Team

#### Email sending flow

Instead of Excel:

You will **copy-paste multiple emails**

Example:

```txt
john@gmail.com
alex@gmail.com
david@gmail.com
mark@gmail.com
```

Or:

```txt
John,john@gmail.com
Alex,alex@gmail.com
David,david@gmail.com
```

System auto-detects and converts into recipients.

### Main Features

#### 1. Login System

* Secure login
* JWT authentication
* Role-based access

**Admin**

* View all accounts
* Manage templates
* See all email histories
* Failed/success emails
* Account health
* Sender statistics
* Activity logs

**User (USA/UK/etc.)**

* Send emails only
* Use assigned templates
* View their own history

* Rich text editor

* Color

* Default saved templates

Dynamic variables:

#### 3. Compose Email Screen

User flow:

1. Select Account (USA/UK)

Features:

Admin dashboard:
